Program Overview

National Specialist advice and Specialised services program for general OPD clinics

There is a pressing need to expand specialist capacities nationwide by enabling experts to diagnose and treat straightforward cases with the help of the doctor at the primary healthcare centre (the general practitioners at the OPD Clinic)

There is also the need to enable OPD clinics to deliver services for the prevention and management of chronic conditions, which most clinics are currently unable to do.

This program aims to empower general OPD clinics nationwide with specialist capabilities and specialist advice programs for the prevention, early diagnosis, local treatment, and timely referral of a wide range of conditions.

Initially, in collaboration with Indian Academy of Pediatrics (IAP) this solution is being offered to their 35,000 members

IAP member clinics across the country will receive resources and tools to enhance growth and development outcomes of children by:

  • Early detection of serious but treatable conditions by providing diagnostic support to the doctors at the point of care, combined with doctor personalized assessment tools and doctor personalized patient education from IAP
  • Local treatment of straightforward cases and timely referral under specialist advice
  • Specialized services from general pediatrics clinics for disease prevention and the management of chronic conditions
  • Training from IAP on red flags and best practices for local therapy under expert advice

Same solution and offering will be extended to all general OPD clinics across India

Currently available services include dietary assessment and meal planning, behavioral and neurodevelopment assessment and specialist advice for Pediatric Pulmonology, Infectious Diseases, Primary Immunodeficiency Diseases, Pediatric Oncology and Pediatric Nutrition.

Specialist Advice System

There is a pressing need to expand specialist capacities nationwide by enabling experts to diagnose and treat straightforward cases with the help of the doctor at the primary healthcare centre (the general OPD Clinic).

This program does not make specialists available to patients. This is a specialist advice system only for the use of doctors.

The Specialist Advice System for General OPD clinics enables early diagnosis, local treatment & management of straightforward cases and timely referrals under specialist advice.

The system includes:

  • Tools to identify red flags that may need specialist advice.
  • Specialist advice for all paediatric subspecialties.

Enabling General OPD clinics with:

  • Capabilities for early diagnosis and local treatment under specialist advice.
  • Timely referrals as advised by the specialist.

The specialist advice system is customized and adapted for pediatrics with IAP’s advice by Protean (formerly NSDL e-Gov), India’s leading e-Governance institution.

This transformative initiative aims to transform India’s healthcare by expanding specialist capacity and bringing specialist care to outpatient clinics

The following specialist advice programs are currently available with more specialties to follow soon:

  • Pediatric Pulmonology
  • Infectious Diseases
  • Primary Immunodeficiency Diseases
  • Pediatric Oncology
  • Pediatric Nutrition

Specialised Services Platform

The ‘Specialised Services’ platform brings disease prevention and management services to all OPD clinics.

The Specialised services may include:

  • Dietary assessments and meal planning.
  • Mental health assessments.
  • Risk assessments and preventive services for lifestyle disease.
  • Services for the management of chronic conditions (genetic conditions, lifestyle diseases, asthma, diabetes, and many others).
  • Services for the management of conditions in special needs children.

The following specialized services are currently available with more to follow soon:

  • Dietary Assessment and meal plan service
  • Behavioural and Neuro-Development
